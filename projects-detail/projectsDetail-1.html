Villa 55

Set in 6th Of October, 6 km from MUST Opera House, Villa 55 provides air-conditioned rooms and a garden. The units in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. Guests at Villa 55 can enjoy a halal breakfast. Mall of Arabia is 100 meters from the accommodation, while Dream Park is 14 km away.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, a shuttle service, room service and free WiFi.. The nearest airport is Sphinx International Airport, 16 km from Villa 55.
